We use cookies on our site to enhance your online experience. Rest assured that the collection of these cookies is carried out in compliance with current confidentiality standards, and your prior consent is always sought for cookies requiring authorization.

When you visit our website, two types of cookies may be collected in order to improve your online experience. In addition, we use third-party cookies issued by external partners, notably for analysis and advertising personalization purposes. Our commitment to your privacy is fundamental, and we invite you to read our full cookie policy to find out more about how cookies are used and how you can exercise control over their management. To summarize, we use :

  • First-party cookies:

         - Issued directly by our website.

         - Issued by external partners.

The collection of data through the use of cookies is entirely optional on our platform. Noodra also attaches great importance to protecting your privacy.

4. How long do cookies stay on your device?

We therefore use two types of cookies on our website: session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary and are automatically deleted as soon as you close your browser. They are essential to ensure smooth browsing during your visit to our site. Persistent cookies, on the other hand, remain on your device even after you close your browser, until they reach their predefined expiry date or are manually deleted by you. These cookies persist in order to store useful information for future visits, improving your experience by offering you faster, more personalized access. We would like to assure you that these cookies are used in accordance with our privacy policy, and you can manage these preferences via your browser settings.

5. Conditions for deleting cookies

Therefore, you are free to adjust the settings of your web browser according to your preferences in terms of cookies with :

  • Google Chrome :

         - Click on the three vertical dots at top right,
         - Select "History" then "History",
         - Click on "Delete browsing data",
         - Check "Cookies and other site data" and click on "Delete data".
  • Mozilla Firefox :

         - Click on the three horizontal lines at top right,
         - Select "Options" then "Privacy & Security",
         - In the "Cookies and website data" section, click on "Delete data...",
         - Check "Cookies" and click "Delete".
  • Microsoft Edge :

         - Click on the three vertical dots at top right,
         -Select "History" then "Delete browsing data",
         - Check "Cookies and website data" and click "Delete".
  • Safari :

         - Click on "History" in the menu bar,
         - Select "Clear history",
         - Select the time range and click on "Clear history".
  • Opera :

         - Click on "Settings" then "Advanced",
         - Select "Privacy & Security" and "Content settings",
         - Choose "Click on cookies",
         - Deactivate "Keep local data only until you exit your browser".
